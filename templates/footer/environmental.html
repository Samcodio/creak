ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY

Aviation Spares & Repairs Ltd is committed to environmental stewardship and sustainable business practices in compliance with ISO 14001 principles and UK environmental legislation.

1. Environmental Commitment

We recognize our responsibility to minimize environmental impact while supporting the aviation industry's sustainability goals and contributing to carbon reduction initiatives.

2. Key Objectives

  • Reduce energy consumption and carbon emissions
  • Minimize waste generation and maximize recycling
  • Promote sustainable supply chain practices
  • Comply with all environmental regulations
  • Continuously improve environmental performance

3. Implementation

We implement environmental management systems, conduct regular assessments, and set measurable targets for improvement. All employees are trained on environmental responsibilities.

4. Supplier Requirements

We work with suppliers who demonstrate environmental responsibility and encourage adoption of sustainable practices throughout our supply chain.

5. Monitoring and Review

Environmental performance is monitored regularly, with annual reviews to assess progress and identify improvement opportunities.
