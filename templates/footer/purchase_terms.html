TERMS & CONDITIONS OF PURCHASE

These terms and conditions govern all purchases made by Aviation Spares & Repairs Ltd from suppliers and form part of our purchase contracts.

1. Supplier Qualification

Suppliers must be approved through our qualification process, maintain appropriate certifications (AS9120, ISO 9001), and comply with aviation industry standards including EASA, FAA, and CAA requirements.

2. Purchase Orders and Acceptance

All purchases require a valid purchase order. Supplier acceptance creates a binding contract. No variations are permitted without written authorization from our procurement department.

3. Quality and Compliance

All goods must comply with specified standards, include proper certification (8130-3, EASA Form 1), and meet airworthiness requirements. Suppliers must maintain full traceability documentation.

4. Delivery and Performance

Time is of the essence. Suppliers must notify us immediately of any potential delays. Late delivery may result in additional costs being charged to the supplier.

5. Inspection and Rejection

We reserve the right to inspect all goods upon receipt. Non-conforming items will be rejected at supplier's cost. Suppliers must provide immediate replacement or credit.

6. Warranties and Indemnification

Suppliers warrant goods are free from defects, fit for purpose, and comply with all specifications. Suppliers indemnify us against claims arising from defective or non-compliant goods.
