ANTI-BRIBERY POLICY

Zero Tolerance: Aviation Spares & Repairs Ltd maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards bribery and corruption in compliance with the UK Bribery Act 2010 and international anti-corruption laws.

1. Policy Statement

We are committed to conducting business with the highest standards of integrity. This policy applies to all employees, directors, agents, and third parties acting on our behalf.

2. Prohibited Activities

The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Offering, giving, requesting, or accepting bribes
  • Facilitation payments to expedite routine services
  • Kickbacks or secret commissions
  • Inappropriate gifts or hospitality
  • Political contributions for business advantage

3. Due Diligence

We conduct appropriate due diligence on business partners, agents, and suppliers to ensure they share our commitment to ethical business practices.

4. Training and Awareness

All personnel receive regular training on anti-bribery requirements and are expected to understand and comply with this policy.

5. Reporting and Investigation

Suspected violations must be reported immediately to management or via our confidential reporting system. All reports will be investigated thoroughly and appropriate action taken.

6. Consequences

Violations may result in disciplinary action including termination, contract cancellation, and referral to law enforcement authorities.
