PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: January 2025 | Effective Date: 1st January 2025

Aviation Spares & Repairs Ltd is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Data Controller

Aviation Spares & Repairs Ltd (Company Registration: 05347543) is the data controller for personal information processed through this website and our business operations.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, address)
  • Company information and job title
  • Purchase history and preferences
  • Website usage data and cookies
  • Communication records

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on legitimate business interests, contractual necessity, legal obligations, and with your consent where required.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill business purposes, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Customer data is typically retained for 7 years in accordance with UK tax and company law requirements.

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data in certain circumstances
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing

6. Contact & Complaints

For privacy queries, contact us at sales@aviationspares.com. You may also lodge complaints with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
